Presented an adaptive nonlinear solver with trust-region globalization designed for discrete fracture network (DFN) reservoir simulation within the operator-based linearisation (OBL) framework. Demonstrated robust convergence on benchmark fractured reservoir cases where standard Newton methods fail.
