Presented the DARTS-based digital twin for geothermal DAPwell systems — combining coupled wellbore–reservoir simulation with real-time sensor data to support operational decision-making for geothermal energy extraction. Demonstrated the digital twin framework on a doublet production scenario with temperature-dependent flow and pressure management.
