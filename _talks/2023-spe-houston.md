Presented research on Fourier Neural Operators and deep learning applied to the operator space of nonlinear PDEs for fast, physics-consistent proxy models of multiphase reservoir simulation. Demonstrated ~10× speedup over classical solvers with comparable physical accuracy, with applications to CO₂ storage and reservoir management.
