Accurate prediction of subsurface energy system behaviour requires tightly coupling **wellbore flow dynamics** with **reservoir simulation**. This work presents a fully coupled wellbore–reservoir model using operator-based linearisation, enabling consistent and convergent solutions across the wellbore–reservoir interface for CO₂ injection and geothermal applications.

**Key contributions:**
- Fully coupled wellbore–reservoir formulation using OBL
- Application to CO₂ injection and geothermal doublet systems
- Validated against field-scale benchmark cases with realistic thermodynamics
