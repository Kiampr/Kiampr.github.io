Discrete fracture network (DFN) models introduce extreme heterogeneity and high nonlinearity into reservoir simulation, making convergence of standard Newton solvers unreliable. This work presents an **adaptive nonlinear solver** with trust-region globalization tailored to the OBL framework, demonstrating robust convergence for DFN cases with high fracture contrast and complex interaction patterns.

**Key contributions:**
- Adaptive step-size control combined with trust-region modification for DFN problems
- Integration within the operator-based linearisation (OBL) simulation framework (DARTS)
- Robust convergence on benchmark fractured reservoir cases
- Presented at ECMOR XVII (European Conference on the Mathematics of Oil Recovery), 2020
